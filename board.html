Board of Directors

The Board of Directors is the highest governing body within FELDA, responsible for the overall strategic direction, oversight, and governance of the organisation. The Board is committed to upholding the highest standards of corporate governance and ensuring that FELDA fulfills its mission effectively.

Board Committees

  • Audit Committee — Oversees financial reporting and internal controls
  • Nomination Committee — Evaluates board composition and appointments
  • Remuneration Committee — Determines compensation policies
  • Risk Management Committee — Monitors enterprise risk management
  • Tender Committee — Oversees procurement and tender processes

Governance

The Board operates in accordance with the Federal Land Development Authority Act and adheres to the Malaysian Code on Corporate Governance. Board meetings are held quarterly, with additional meetings convened as necessary to address urgent matters.
