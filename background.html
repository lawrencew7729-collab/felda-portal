Corporate Background

FELDA has evolved from a land development agency into a diversified corporation with interests in plantation management, downstream processing, property development, and logistics. Today, FELDA manages some of the largest oil palm estates in the world and has a significant presence in the global vegetable oils market.

The corporatisation of FELDA in recent years has enabled the organisation to operate more efficiently, leveraging its vast land bank and expertise to create sustainable value for its stakeholders.

Core Business Activities

  • Plantation Management: Managing over 800,000 hectares of oil palm and rubber plantations
  • Downstream Processing: Palm oil refining, oleochemicals, and specialty fats
  • Property Development: Developing FELDA's land bank for commercial and residential use
  • Logistics: Transportation and supply chain management for agricultural produce
  • Research & Development: Agricultural research and innovation for sustainable farming

Corporate Structure

FELDA operates through its headquarters at FELDA Tower, Kuala Lumpur, with regional offices throughout Malaysia. The organisation is led by a Board of Directors and managed by a team of experienced professionals across various divisions.
